Puntros Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Puntros (the “App”) handles information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account data: basic profile details such as name, phone number, and role used for sign-in and access control.
  • Usage data: app interactions (e.g., creating orders, updating inventory) used to operate core features.
  • Device data: non-sensitive device info (e.g., OS version) collected only to improve stability.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain core functionality (order capture, inventory, reporting).
  • To secure accounts and prevent unauthorized access.
  • To analyze performance and improve reliability.

Sharing

We do not sell user data. Information is shared only with service providers needed to run the App (for example, cloud hosting/database) under similar confidentiality safeguards.

Data Retention

We keep data for as long as your account remains active or as needed to operate the App. You may request deletion of your account data (see Contact).

Security

We use industry-standard security practices (encrypted transport, access controls). No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure; we strive to protect data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

  • You can update or correct profile information within the App.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be noted with a new effective date. Continued use of the App means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

For questions or data requests, contact: privacy@puntros.app
